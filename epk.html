Bio

Canadian singer/songwriter, Joel Guptill, is a Nashville-based artist whose heartfelt lyrics, refreshing melodies, and engaging performances have captivated audiences across the US and Canada. With influences from the likes of John Mayer, Theo Katzman, and Allen Stone, his music is a healthy blend of rock, groove and soulful blues. Joel released his latest single, “How Do I Break” in the spring of 2021, and is currently working on his next project set to release in the spring of 2023.
